Delicious Fried Rice

Easy homemade recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Vegetables (carrot, peas)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Add garlic and fry until fragrant
  3. Add egg and scramble
  4. Add rice and mix well
  5. Add soy sauce and vegetables
  6. Cook for 5 minutes and serve
